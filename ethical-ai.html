Ethical AI

How we use AI responsibly in school work and projects — with safety, fairness, and integrity.

Safety & Privacy

Avoid sharing personal data or secrets with AI tools. Remove identifiers and follow school policy.

Fairness & Bias

AI can reflect biased data. Cross‑check outputs, seek diverse sources, and acknowledge limitations.

Integrity & Attribution

Use AI for support, not to replace your work. Paraphrase, cite sources, and disclose assistance when required.

Good Practices

  • Ask for outlines and explanations rather than finished essays.
  • Request sources and verify them; avoid copy‑paste.
  • Keep prompts safe — no private or sensitive data.
  • Use AI to debug, brainstorm, and learn concepts.

What to Avoid

  • Submitting AI‑generated text as your own work.
  • Sharing personal data or credentials in prompts.
  • Bypassing rules, cheating, or generating harmful content.
  • Relying on a single AI answer without verification.
