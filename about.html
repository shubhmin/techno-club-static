About the Club

We’re a student‑run club at DAV Public School, Sreshtha Vihar, focused on cyber hygiene, safe browsing, secure coding, and spreading awareness.

Our activities include biweekly meets, guest talks by alumni, and collaborative projects.

  • Workshops (passwords, 2FA, phishing)
  • Student‑built tools & websites
  • Annual awareness drive
  • Ethical AI practices & prompt engineering
