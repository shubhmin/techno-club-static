About the Club

We’re a student‑run club at DAV Public School, Sreshtha Vihar, focused on cyber hygiene, safe browsing, secure coding, and spreading awareness.

Our activities include weekly meets, guest talks by alumni, and collaborative projects.

  • Workshops (passwords, 2FA, phishing)
  • Student‑built tools & websites
  • Annual awareness drive

Our Mission

Build a culture of cyber safety and ethical AI use through hands‑on learning and peer awareness.

Our Approach

Learn by doing — labs, mini‑projects, friendly competitions, and responsible AI practices.

Our Values

Curiosity, responsibility, fairness, transparency, teamwork, and impact.
